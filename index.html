How to read the score

Completion is a present-day capability score, not a prediction.

A high score does not mean the fictional version exists. It means the closest real-world capability has crossed more of the physics, prototype, deployment, and manufacturing thresholds.

Why this exists

A public engineering thought experiment.

This atlas asks what happens when imagined futures are evaluated through manufacturability, scale-up, reliability, evidence quality, and process bottlenecks instead of pure aesthetic appeal. The goal is not exact prophecy; the goal is to make technology readiness legible.
